# AegisIDE Business Model & Monetization Strategy

## Core Business Philosophy

### Free-First Local Architecture
**Fundamental Principle**: AegisIDE core remains **100% free** with local operation
- No subscription fees for basic functionality
- Zero ongoing costs for individual developers
- Complete feature access without payment barriers
- Privacy-first with local-only data processing

**Value Creation Strategy**: Generate revenue through **value-added services** rather than feature restrictions
