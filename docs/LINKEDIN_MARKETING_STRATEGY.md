## Measurement & Analytics Strategy

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Engagement Metrics**:
- **Reach**: Unique LinkedIn users seeing content (target: 50K+ monthly)
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares per post (target: 8%+)
- **Click-Through Rate**: Links to AegisIDE content (target: 3%+)
- **Follower Growth**: New followers per month (target: 1000+)

**Conversion Metrics**:
- **Beta Sign-ups**: LinkedIn-attributed registrations (target: 500+ monthly)
- **Enterprise Inquiries**: Sales leads from LinkedIn (target: 10+ monthly)
- **Community Joins**: Discord/GitHub community growth (target: 200+ monthly)
- **Email Subscribers**: Newsletter sign-ups from LinkedIn (target: 300+ monthly)

**Quality Metrics**:
- **Developer Engagement**: Comments from actual developers vs general audience
- **Share Quality**: Shares with thoughtful commentary vs simple reshares
- **Inbound Messages**: Direct messages from potential users and partners
- **Content Virality**: Posts shared beyond immediate network

### A/B Testing Framework

**Content Format Testing**:
- **Post Length**: Short vs long-form content performance
- **Visual Elements**: Carousels vs single images vs videos
- **Call-to-Action**: Different CTA phrases and placement
- **Posting Times**: Optimal times for developer audience engagement

**Message Testing**:
- **Pain Point Focus**: Cost vs privacy vs productivity messaging
- **Solution Emphasis**: Constitutional governance vs local-first vs multi-IDE
- **Audience Targeting**: Individual developers vs enterprise decision makers
- **Tone**: Technical vs business-focused vs community-oriented
